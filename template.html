My Name

I'm Min Yoon-gi; born March 9, 1993, known professionally by his stage names Sugaa; stylized in all caps and Agust D,i'm a South Korean rapper, songwriter and record producer. Under Big Hit Entertainment,i debuted as a member of the South Korean boy band BTS in 2013.My first solo mixtape, Agust D, was released in 2016 and re-released in 2018 to digital download and streaming platforms, reaching number three on Billboard's World Albums Chart. In 2020,i released my second solo mixtape, D-2; it peaked at number 11 on the US Billboard 200, number seven on the UK Albums Chart, and number two on Australia's ARIA Album Chart.
